<objective>
Define the five entity schemas (product, ingredient, brand, additive) plus TimelineEvent and the image-rights record, so ingestion has somewhere to field-tag into (DATA-11). TimelineEvent keeps documented change, stated reason and labelled inference separate (DATA-04). The image schema gives the DATA-10 gate a rights status to enforce. A consistency test guards the DATA-09 designation and the vocabulary sync.

Purpose: These are the shapes the whole project writes into. Defining them before ingestion is the sequencing decision the roadmap locked.
Output: six schema files under schemas/, plus a schema-consistency test.
</objective>
