<objective>
Build the two text/asset gates wired into prebuild by plan 01-01: the image-rights default-deny gate (DATA-10) and the British-English plus neutral-editorial lint (UX-06). The editorial lint is SCOPED: Class A (em-dash, en-GB spellings, sentence-case warning) applies everywhere; Class B (superlatives, denigratory and motive phrases) applies ONLY to analyst-authored fields and prose, never to attributed-quote fields, because DATA-04/PROD-08 expressly permit a manufacturer's attributed stated reason and a labelled inference. Each gate ships with negative fixtures and a passing attributed-quote fixture. The Phase 4 data-level no-imputation-of-motive publication gate is NOT built here; Phase 1 ships the text lint only.

Purpose: Default-deny on imagery and a neutral British editorial voice become structural invariants before any content exists, WITHOUT banning lawful attributed quotation.
Output: lib/check-images.mjs, scripts/check-images.mjs, images.json, lib/editorial-rules.mjs, scripts/check-editorial.mjs, two test files, the fixtures.
</objective>
