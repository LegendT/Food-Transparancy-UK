<objective>
Wire the Phase 2 per-fact verification gate into the existing offline validation harness (`scripts/validate-data.mjs`), extending the Phase 1 gate chain. The gate imports the pure checks from `lib/verification.mjs`, reads the committed `.cache/citation-verdicts.json` (never the network, Pitfall 3), adds the distinct-lineage and measure-mismatch checks to the build-failing error chain, and asserts each fact's `verification` record is internally consistent (`contested` requires `positions`; passes' `sourcesChecked` are a subset of the fact's `sources`; `markedWrong` well-formed). Critically, a WITHHELD fact is the normal correct outcome and does NOT fail the build; only structural and internal-consistency violations do.

Purpose: This is the moment the trust layer becomes a build invariant. A fact that cannot meet its claim standard is withheld, not asserted, on every build (VRFY-04 continuous).
Output: The extended gate and the end-to-end corpus-gate tests. This plan owns scripts/validate-data.mjs exclusively.
</objective>
